Tips and Tricks to make you like a pro!

Here are a few things that you can start working on to give you the skills you need to be a surfing pro!

  1. Paddle deep and long strokes
    • This will allow for more power and greater chance to catch the wave
    • Small strokes will do nothing but splash water everywhere!
  2. Know when the wave is about to take you!
    • You will feel yourself and the board gaining speed
    • That is when you have to stand up!
  3. How to stand up
    • Simply do a pushup!
    • Use your arms to push your body up onto your legs and spring up like standing from a squat!
    • Don't forget to balance yourself by widening your stance and putting your arms out!
